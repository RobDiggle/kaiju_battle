KAIJU BATTLE

In a world pushed to the brink of annihilation, ten Kaiju emerge to settle an ancient score. Cities tremble, skies darken, and only one monster will stand victorious when the dust clears. Choose wisely — the fate of everything rests on this battle.
In this game you will pick a Kaiju monster and battle an opposing player. Each of the Kaiju below has an ATTACK LEVEL, HEALTH, and a SPECIAL move. In alternating turns two players can ATTACK, DEFEND or employ a SPECIAL move until victory is achieved. Click on information to see the relative stats of any of the playable Kaiju characters and then choose your Kaiju to begin the battle!
